Working with Macros

1. Question 1
You need to change the shortcut key that starts your macro. How do you do this?
1.Go to the Developer tab, click on Macros, then choose Options.	
2. Question 2
You can copy and paste lines of code from one macro to another in the VBA Editor.
2.True
3. Question 3
How do you delete an existing macro in a workbook?
3.Go to the Developer tab, click on Macros, then choose Delete.
