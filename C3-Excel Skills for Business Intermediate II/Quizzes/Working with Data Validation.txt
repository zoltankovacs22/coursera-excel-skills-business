Working with Data Validation

1. Question 1
How do you clear validation from a cell (or cells)?
1.Select the cell/s, click on Clear all in the Data Validation dialog.
2. Question 2
After applying Data Validation to a cell (or cells):
(One or more answers are possible. Partial credit will be awarded)

Excel can highlight pre-existing invalid data with red circles.
3. Question 3
How can you identify cells which contain the same data validation as a currently selected cell?
3.Go to the Home tab, click on Find and Select, then choose Go to Special - Data Validation, Same.
