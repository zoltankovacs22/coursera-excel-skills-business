Run your Macro

1. Question 1
Why does Excel check with you first before it enables macros upon opening a file?
1.The default security settings in Excel check this as macros may contain harmful code and might do damage to your files and your computer.
2. Question 2
Where can you find and adjust the settings for how Excel handles macros?
2.Go to the Developer tab and locate the Code group, then click on Macro Security.
Go to the Backstage and locate the Trust Center in the Options menu.
3. Question 3
How can you adjust the security settings for macros without exposing yourself to unnecessary risks?
3.Leave the default Macro Security settings as they are and create a Trusted Location for your files with macros instead.
